# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 15, 2026

Mental Mantra ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Personal Information**: When you create an account or sign in, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication tokens provided by Firebase Authentication.
- **Health & Wellness Data**: Any journal entries, mood logs, urges, or recovery scores you voluntarily input are stored securely in Firestore. This data is used solely to provide personalized AI coaching and analytics.
- **Device Information**: We collect device identifiers, operating system version, and crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability.
- **Usage Analytics**: Interaction events (e.g., track plays, background theme selections) are captured through Firebase Analytics for product improvement.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To deliver personalized AI‑driven mental‑wellness coaching.
- To generate session summaries, insights, and recommendations.
- To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

## 3. Data Sharing & Disclosure
- **Third‑Party Services**: We use Google Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics) that may process your data in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.
- **Aggregated Data**: Aggregated, non‑identifiable data may be used for research or marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Security
- All data in transit is encrypted with HTTPS/TLS.
- Data at rest in Firestore is encrypted by Google Cloud.
- Access is restricted to authenticated users and service accounts with least‑privilege permissions.

## 5. Your Rights
- **Access & Correction**: You may view, edit, or delete your personal data via the app settings.
- **Deletion**: Deleting your account removes all associated data from Firestore.
- **Export**: You can request a copy of your data by contacting support.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and will become effective upon publication.

**Contact Us**
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email support@mentalmantra.app.
